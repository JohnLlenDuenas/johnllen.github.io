Consent Agreement

ㅤI, the undersigned, am the parent/legal guardian of the student named below. I understand that as part of the Online Yearbook Management System, my child’s photograph and related content may be displayed on the school’s official website and shared across various social media platforms.

Revocation of Consent: I understand that I may revoke this consent at any time by providing written notice to the school. However, I acknowledge that content already published cannot be retracted.

Release of Liability: I release Malanday National High School and its representatives from any liability that may arise from the use of my child’s photographs as described in this consent form.
